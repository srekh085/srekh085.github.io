Our Story

Bike Shire was born out of our passion for cycling. With dozens of two-wheeled adventures behind us, we decided to help other riders go on their own. We have overcome many obstacles over thousands of kilometeres, so rest assured, our bikes are made to last.

Every member of our Team is an avid rider! Hop in anytime for a repair, a new bike, or a story!
